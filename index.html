Who We Are

Specialists in significant change

Vivalto is a specialist company focusing on projects and programmes that deliver significant change. We work with organisations across the public and private sectors, bringing deep practical experience to complex transformation challenges.

Change has many parts but it's people that make it happen

We understand that every transformation programme is different — shaped by the organisation, its people, and the particular challenge it faces. That is why we bring a hands-on, collaborative approach, working alongside your teams rather than at arm's length.

Our principal consultants have extensive track records delivering complex change programmes spanning local government, central government, utilities, financial services, rail, housing, retail, and the wider private sector. We combine specialist accounting, systems, and commercial expertise with the programme management rigour and people skills that major change demands.

Vivalto LLP is registered in England and Wales.

What We Do

Specialist expertise across the full change lifecycle

We focus on the disciplines that drive transformation — bringing specialist knowledge and proven delivery experience to the projects and programmes that matter most.

01 — ERP & Systems

ERP & Systems Implementation

Planning and delivering large-scale ERP and systems implementations with the accounting rigour and programme management discipline they demand. We ensure technology programmes stay grounded in operational and commercial reality.

02 — Public Sector

Public Sector Transformation

Deep experience in local and central government change — from social care transformation and public sector cost reduction to s151 management, shared services, and housing and waste strategies.

03 — Systems Change

Systems-Led Transformation

Where systems change drives wider organisational transformation, we provide specialist leadership to manage the full programme — technology, process, people, and commercial outcomes brought together.

04 — Governance

Programme Governance & Audit

Robust governance frameworks and internal operational audit to give leadership genuine visibility of progress, risk, and financial performance — turning the complexity of major programmes into clarity and control.

05 — Data

Data Migration & Reporting

From complex data migration through systems change to Big Data reporting strategies — and from customer segmentation to management information — we help organisations make their data work.

06 — Commercial

Commercial & Contract

Contract drafting, negotiation, mergers, demergers, and acquisitions — as well as sales commission and incentive scheme design. Commercial rigour for the transactions and agreements that underpin change.

Our Approach

Rigorous thinking.
Practical delivery.

At Vivalto we believe that significant change requires both deep specialist knowledge and a pragmatic, disciplined approach to delivery. We combine technical expertise with the people skills that real transformation demands.

Change has many parts — systems, processes, data, contracts, governance — but it's people who make it happen. Our approach always keeps that truth at its centre, working alongside your teams and adapting to the culture and context of your organisation.

We are not a large generalist firm. We are a focused specialist practice that brings senior-level commitment and accountability to every engagement.

01
Understand

We invest the time to genuinely understand your organisation, your people, and the full complexity of the challenge before recommending any course of action.

02
Plan

A clear, realistic plan — with transparent assumptions, defined milestones, and honest risk management — forms the foundation of every programme we support.

03
Deliver

We bring specialist resource and hands-on commitment to delivery, maintaining the momentum and discipline that complex programmes require to succeed.

04
Sustain

We help embed lasting change — ensuring the capabilities, controls, and ways of working put in place continue to deliver value well beyond programme close.
